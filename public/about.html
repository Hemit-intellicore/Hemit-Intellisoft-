Who We Are

Hemit Intellisoft Ltd is a leading technology company specializing in renewable energy solutions, high-performance computing, and premium mobile devices. Founded in 2015, we have grown from a small startup to a trusted name in the tech industry, serving thousands of customers across Africa and beyond.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing cutting-edge technology at affordable prices. We believe in sustainable innovation that empowers businesses and individuals to thrive in the digital age.

With a team of 50+ dedicated professionals and partnerships with global tech leaders, we continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in solar technology, computing power, and mobile innovation.

Our Journey

January 2015

Company Founded

Hemit Intellisoft Ltd was established in Lagos, Nigeria with a vision to revolutionize renewable energy and computing solutions across Africa.
June 2016

First Solar Product Launch

Launched our first hybrid solar inverter, bringing reliable clean energy to over 500 homes in Nigeria within the first year.
March 2018

Expanded to Computing

Entered the high-performance computing market with gaming PCs and workstations, establishing partnerships with Intel and NVIDIA.
November 2020

Mobile Division Launch

Introduced premium mobile devices division, becoming official distributor for leading smartphone brands across West Africa.
April 2023

10,000th Customer

Celebrated serving our 10,000th customer and expanded operations to 5 African countries with new regional offices.
2025

Global Expansion

Launching international operations and smart energy management systems to serve emerging markets worldwide.
