Refund Policy

1. Overview

At Hemit Intellisoft Ltd, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction with our technology solutions, including web development, mobile applications, cloud computing, and AI & automation services. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be requested for our services. By engaging with our services, you agree to this policy.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • Non-Delivery: If we fail to deliver the agreed-upon service within the timeline specified in the project agreement.
  • Service Failure: If the delivered service does not meet the specifications outlined in the project agreement or is non-functional due to errors on our part.
  • Cancellation: If you cancel a service before work has commenced, subject to any deposit or initial payment terms.

Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of service delivery or cancellation.

3. Non-Refundable Services

The following are generally non-refundable:

  • Completed Work: Services that have been fully delivered and accepted by you.
  • Custom Development: Custom software, websites, or applications developed to your specifications, unless they fail to meet agreed standards.
  • Deposits: Initial deposits or progress payments for ongoing projects, unless otherwise specified in the project agreement.
  • Third-Party Fees: Fees paid to third-party providers (e.g., hosting, domain registration) on your behalf.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  • Contact us at info@hemitintellisoft.com with your project details, reason for the refund request, and any supporting documentation.
  • We will review your request within 7 business days and may request additional information.
  • If approved, refunds will be processed within 14 business days to the original payment method or as agreed.

5. Exceptions

We may offer partial refunds or alternative remedies (e.g., service revisions) at our discretion if the issue can be resolved without a full refund. For subscription-based services, refunds for unused periods may be prorated based on the subscription terms.

6. Compliance with Laws

Our Refund Policy complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and applicable consumer protection laws in Nigeria. For users in the EEA, additional rights under GDPR may apply, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
